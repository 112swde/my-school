activities

inter-class cricket competition

held every year in the first quarter.

The Salt Brook School cricket tournament is an action packed and exciting event.Played between classes 6 to 9, this event attracts quite a number of spectators. This competition i held in the school ground. This year, class 9 won. Myself, Ayushman, had also participated representing class 6. Our team did pretty well. We defeated class 9 too in the first half of the tournament but in the second half theywere fully dominant.



Science and Art exihibition

held on every odd year (like- 2015, 2019 and 2021)

This event is just amazing. The students of Salt Brook School showcase some of their best works of science and art. The most iconic and mesmerizing exihibits were-
1.The Li-Fi machine(2019) Made by class 10. mmm...how do i describe it. It's out of my mind.
2.A.I robot(2019) Made by class 8. The robot can speak and draw but cannot walk, because it only has its body till its abdomen. No legs.
These are the ones which truly amazed me but all the exihibits are created through hard work of the students
